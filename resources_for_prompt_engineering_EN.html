Resources for Prompt Engineering

Thibaut Lombard

Resource Description Link
PromptHero A community platform to share and discover optimized prompts for Midjourney, DALL·E, and other generative AIs. https://prompthero.com/
PromptBase A marketplace to buy and sell tested, high-performing prompts for various AI models. https://promptbase.com/
FlowGPT A community where users share free prompts for ChatGPT and test the best ideas together. https://flowgpt.com/
PromptingGuide.ai An educational guide to learn prompt engineering principles and explore examples. https://promptingguide.ai/
Reddit - r/ChatGPT A forum for discussions, prompt sharing, and feedback on ChatGPT usage. https://www.reddit.com/r/ChatGPT/
Reddit - r/PromptEngineering A subreddit to exchange about prompt engineering techniques and share advanced tips. https://www.reddit.com/r/PromptEngineering/
Awesome ChatGPT Prompts (GitHub) An open-source collection of creative and technical prompts to get the best out of ChatGPT. https://github.com/f/awesome-chatgpt-prompts
Prompt Engineering Guide (GitHub) A complete guide on prompt engineering, including theory and hands-on resources. https://github.com/dair-ai/Prompt-Engineering-Guide
Heuristics - Nielsen Norman Group A key resource on UX heuristics, including Jakob Nielsen’s 10 usability heuristics. https://www.nngroup.com/articles/ten-usability-heuristics/
Heuristics - Interaction Design Foundation Detailed explanations and examples of heuristic evaluation for design and AI projects. https://www.interaction-design.org/literature/topics/heuristic-evaluation
